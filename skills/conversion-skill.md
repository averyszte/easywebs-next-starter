# Conversion Skill

For the app's public and onboarding pages — generate trust and action.

## CTA

- Clear primary CTA above the fold; natural wording; one dominant action per screen; reduce form friction.

## Trust

- Reviews/testimonials, real imagery, clear contact details, consistent branding.

## UX

- Fast load, mobile-first, clear navigation, an easy sign-up / inquiry path.

## Avoid

- Overwhelming users, too many CTAs, long unnecessary forms, generic marketing buzzwords.
